This is the final project of the subject. It consists on an interactive 3d endless running game. You can play moving your body up,down, and to the sides while you are looking to the camera.
To execute:
1. open windows bash in posenetosc-master and type node bridge.js. Press enter.
2. open windows bash in posenetosc-master and type yarn watch. Press enter.
3. a new window on your computer will open asking for camera permission.
1. open the unity project and play (the posnet window has to be opened).
